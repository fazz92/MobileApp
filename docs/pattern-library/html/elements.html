Classes for hiding and showing elements based on viewport

.s-hide

Hides element in small viewport. Element is shown in medium, large and xlarge viewports.

.m-hide

Hides element in small and medium viewports. Element is shown large and xlarge viewports.

.l-hide

Hides element in large and xlarge viewports. Element in shown small in medium viewports.

.s-show

Shows element in small viewport only.

Example

Resize window to show/hide paragraphs.

.s-hide >> I hide in small viewport. I show in medium, large and x-large.

.m-hide >> I hide in small and medium viewports. I show in large and x-large.

.l-hide >> I hide in large and xlarge viewports. I show in small and medium.

.s-show >> I hide in medium, large and xlarge viewports. I show only in small viewport.
